Introduction

Calendar problems are based on counting days, months, and years. The main idea is to determine the day of the week for any given date using leap year rules and odd days method.

Concept Explanation

Every week has 7 days, so we calculate extra days beyond complete weeks (called odd days). These odd days help determine the weekday.

Key Idea: Weekday depends on total odd days after dividing by 7

Leap Year Rules

  • Divisible by 4 → Leap year
  • Not divisible by 100 unless divisible by 400
  • Leap year has 366 days

Odd Days Concept

  • 1 week = 7 days = 0 odd days
  • Remaining days after full weeks = odd days
  • Odd days help determine weekday

Weekday Mapping

0 = Sunday
1 = Monday
2 = Tuesday
3 = Wednesday
4 = Thursday
5 = Friday
6 = Saturday

Shortcut Tricks

  • Break years into 400-year cycles (pattern repeats)
  • Use modulo 7 for fast calculation
  • Memorize leap year rules clearly
  • Convert months into odd days quickly

Solved Example

Answer: Friday
