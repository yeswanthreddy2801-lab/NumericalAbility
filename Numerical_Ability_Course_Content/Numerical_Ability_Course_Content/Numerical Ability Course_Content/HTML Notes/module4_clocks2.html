Clocks - Part 2

Learning Objectives

  • Understand mirror clock concept
  • Learn gain and loss problems
  • Solve advanced clock reasoning

Mirror Concept

Mirror Time = 11:60 - Given Time

Example: 3:15 → 8:45

Clock Gain/Loss

✔ Gain = Fast clock

✔ Loss = Slow clock

✔ Measured per day

Important Rules

  • Use 11:60 rule for mirror problems
  • Calculate error based on days
  • Compare real vs shown time

Placement Tips

  • Memorize mirror formula
  • Practice time correction problems
  • Identify fast/slow clock quickly

Quick Summary

Clocks Part 2 focuses on mirror images and clock errors. Always apply correction logic carefully.
