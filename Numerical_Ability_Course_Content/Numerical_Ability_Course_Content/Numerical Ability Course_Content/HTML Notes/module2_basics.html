Basics of Permutations & Combinations

Foundation concepts for counting problems in aptitude

Learning Objectives

  • Understand counting principle
  • Learn factorial concept
  • Identify permutation vs combination
  • Simplify factorial expressions

Fundamental Principle

If one task has m ways and another has n ways, total ways = m × n.

Factorial Concept

n! = n × (n−1) × ... × 1

0! = 1

5! = 120

Shortcut Techniques

  • Break factorial before multiplying
  • Always check order matters or not
  • Use multiplication rule for counting

Solved Example

7!/5! = 7×6 = 42
