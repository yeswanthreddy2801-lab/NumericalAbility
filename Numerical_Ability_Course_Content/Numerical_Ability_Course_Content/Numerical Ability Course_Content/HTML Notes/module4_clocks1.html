Clocks - Part 1

Learning Objectives

  • Understand basic clock concepts
  • Learn angle calculation between hands
  • Solve time-based aptitude questions

Key Concepts

✔ 1 hour = 30°

✔ 1 minute = 6°

✔ Hour hand moves 0.5° per minute

Formula

Angle = |(30 × Hour) - (11/2 × Minutes)|

Solved Example

Time: 3:20

Angle = |(30×3) - (11/2×20)|

= |90 - 110| = 20°

Placement Tips

  • Always check hour & minute positions first
  • Use formula directly for speed
  • Practice angle-based questions daily

Quick Summary

Clock problems are based on angle difference between hour and minute hands. Use standard formula for fast calculation.
