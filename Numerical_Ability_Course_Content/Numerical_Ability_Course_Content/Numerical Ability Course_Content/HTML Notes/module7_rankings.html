Rankings

Learning Objectives

  • Understand position-based problems
  • Solve left-right ranking questions
  • Use shortcut formulas

Introduction

Ranking problems are based on positions of people in a line or arrangement.

Key Formula

Total = Left Rank + Right Rank - 1

Shortcut Tricks

  • Add both ranks and subtract 1
  • Reverse position when needed
  • Draw line for clarity

Solved Example

Example: 3rd from left, 5th from right

Total = 3 + 5 - 1 = 7

Placement Tips

  • Always visualize line arrangement
  • Use formula directly in exams
  • Avoid confusion by marking positions

Quick Summary

Ranking = position logic + simple addition formula.
