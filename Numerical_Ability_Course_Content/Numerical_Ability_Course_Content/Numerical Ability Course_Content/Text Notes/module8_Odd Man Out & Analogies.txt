INTRODUCTION

Odd Man Out and Analogies are important topics in reasoning aptitude. These questions test your ability to identify patterns, relationships, and differences between given items.

============================================================

ODD MAN OUT CONCEPT

In Odd Man Out questions:
• A group of items is given
• All items follow a pattern except one
• You must identify the different one

Types:
1. Number-based
2. Alphabet-based
3. Word-based
4. Mixed pattern

============================================================

ANALOGY CONCEPT

Analogy means relationship between two elements.

Format:
A : B :: C : ?

You must find relationship between A and B, then apply same to C.

============================================================

IMPORTANT RELATION TYPES

• Synonyms / Antonyms
• Number patterns
• Letter shifting
• Group classification
• Mathematical operations
• Word meaning relationship

============================================================

SHORTCUT TECHNIQUES

• Look for pattern first, not calculation
• Compare differences, ratios, or sequences
• Eliminate obvious wrong options
• Check alphabetical positions quickly
• Practice common analogy pairs

============================================================

SOLVED EXAMPLE 1 (ODD MAN OUT)

2, 4, 8, 16, 18

Answer: 18 (not power of 2)

============================================================

SOLVED EXAMPLE 2 (ANALOGY)

Book : Reading :: Fork : ?

Answer: Eating
