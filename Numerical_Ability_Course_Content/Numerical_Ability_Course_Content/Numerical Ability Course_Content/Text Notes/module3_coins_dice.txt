PROBABILITY - COINS & DICE

--------------------------------------------------

## Introduction

Probability is the measure of chances of an event occurring.

Formula:
Probability = Favorable Outcomes / Total Outcomes

Value always lies between:
0 and 1

--------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------

## Coin Basics

One coin has 2 outcomes:
• Head (H)
• Tail (T)

Two coins outcomes:
HH, HT, TH, TT → 4 outcomes

Formula:
Total outcomes = 2^n

Where n = number of coins

--------------------------------------------------

## Dice Basics

One dice has 6 outcomes:
1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6

Two dice outcomes:
6 × 6 = 36 outcomes

Formula:
Total outcomes = 6^n

--------------------------------------------------

## Key Probability Types

### 1. Simple Event
One outcome event

### 2. Compound Event
Multiple outcomes event

### 3. Sure Event
Probability = 1

### 4. Impossible Event
Probability = 0

--------------------------------------------------

## Example 1 (Coin)

Probability of getting Head when one coin is tossed:

= 1/2

--------------------------------------------------

## Example 2 (Two Coins)

Probability of getting at least one Head:

Favorable: HH, HT, TH = 3

Total = 4

Probability = 3/4

--------------------------------------------------

## Example 3 (Dice)

Probability of getting number 5:

= 1/6

--------------------------------------------------

## Example 4 (Two Dice)

Probability of sum = 7:

Favorable pairs:
(1,6),(2,5),(3,4),(4,3),(5,2),(6,1)

= 6/36 = 1/6
