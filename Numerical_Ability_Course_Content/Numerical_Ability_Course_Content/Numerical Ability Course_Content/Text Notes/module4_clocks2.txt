CLOCKS (PART 2 - MEDIUM LEVEL)

============================================================

1. INTRODUCTION

This section covers medium-level clock concepts like mirror images, simple angle questions, time calculations, and basic clock properties.

============================================================

2. MIRROR TIME (BASIC RULE)

Mirror Time = 11:60 - Original Time

Steps:
• Subtract hour from 11
• Subtract minutes from 60

Example:
Time = 4:20
Mirror Time = 7:40

============================================================

3. BASIC ANGLE FACT

Angle between hands:
Angle = |30H - (11/2)M|

Used for finding angle between hour and minute hands.

============================================================

4. COMMON ANGLES

• 12:00 → 0°
• 3:00 → 90°
• 6:00 → 180°
• 9:00 → 270°

============================================================

5. HAND MOVEMENT SPEED

Minute hand:
• 6° per minute

Hour hand:
• 0.5° per minute

Difference:
• 5.5° per minute

============================================================

6. TIME WHEN HANDS COINCIDE

Hands coincide when both hands are at same position.

They coincide approximately every:
• 65 5/11 minutes

============================================================

7. TIME WHEN HANDS ARE OPPOSITE

Hands are opposite when angle = 180°.

This happens a few times in 12 hours.

============================================================

8. SIMPLE CLOCK FACTS

• Clock has 12 hours on dial
• 360° full rotation
• Each hour = 30°

============================================================

9. QUICK SHORTCUTS

• Convert time into H and M clearly
• Use angle formula directly
• Memorize 30H rule
• Practice mirror formula regularly

============================================================

10. COMMON QUESTION TYPES

• Find angle between hands
• Mirror image of time
• Time when hands coincide
• Simple direction of hands movement

============================================================

11. IMPORTANT POINT

Hour hand moves slowly compared to minute hand.
Minute hand is 12 times faster than hour hand.
