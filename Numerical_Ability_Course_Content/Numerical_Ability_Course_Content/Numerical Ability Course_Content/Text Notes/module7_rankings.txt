============================================================
RANKINGS - FLASH CARDS (BASIC CONCEPTS)
============================================================

1. WHAT IS RANKING?

Ranking means arranging persons/objects based on position
from left or right.

------------------------------------------------------------

2. BASIC TERMS

Left Rank  → Position counted from left side
Right Rank → Position counted from right side

------------------------------------------------------------

3. IMPORTANT FORMULA

Total Persons = Left Rank + Right Rank - 1

------------------------------------------------------------

4. KEY POINTS

• Always check direction (left or right)
• Position starts from 1, not 0
• Total count is always fixed in ranking problems

------------------------------------------------------------

5. COMMON QUESTION TYPES

• Finding total number of persons
• Finding position from opposite side
• Interchanging left and right ranks
• Ranking in a queue
• Missing person problems

------------------------------------------------------------

6. SHORTCUT TRICK

If you know:
Left rank = L
Right rank = R

Then:
Total = L + R - 1

------------------------------------------------------------

7. EXAMPLE

A person is 4th from left and 6th from right.

Total persons = 4 + 6 - 1 = 9

------------------------------------------------------------

8. COMMON MISTAKES

• Forgetting to subtract 1
• Confusing left and right
• Starting count from 0
• Not fixing direction properly

------------------------------------------------------------

9. REAL LIFE APPLICATIONS

• Exam seating arrangement
• Competition rankings
• Queue systems (banks, counters)
• Sports rankings
• Roll number arrangements

------------------------------------------------------------

10. QUICK PRACTICE POINTS

• Always draw a line for people
• Mark left and right clearly
• Use formula directly when possible
• Verify answer logically
