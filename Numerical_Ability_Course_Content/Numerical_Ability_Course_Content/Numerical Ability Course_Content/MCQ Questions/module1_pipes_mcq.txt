PIPES AND CISTERNS - MCQ QUESTIONS

1. Pipe A fills a tank in 12 hours. Its one-hour work is:
A) 12
B) 1/12
C) 1/10
D) 10
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

2. A pipe fills a tank in 20 hours. In 5 hours it fills:
A) 1/2
B) 1/4
C) 1/5
D) 1/6
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

3. Pipe A fills in 10 hours and Pipe B fills in 15 hours. Together they take:
A) 6 hours
B) 7.5 hours
C) 8 hours
D) 5 hours
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

4. A pipe empties a tank in 24 hours. Its rate is:
A) 1/24
B) -1/24
C) 24
D) -24
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

5. A fills in 8 hours, B empties in 16 hours. Combined time:
A) 16 hours
B) 12 hours
C) 10 hours
D) 8 hours
Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

6. Outlet pipe is treated as:
A) Positive work
B) Negative work
C) Zero work
D) Double work
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

7. A fills in 6 hours, B fills in 12 hours. Together:
A) 4 hours
B) 3 hours
C) 6 hours
D) 8 hours
Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

8. If a pipe fills in N hours, one-hour work is:
A) N
B) 1/N
C) N/2
D) 2/N
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

9. A and B fill a tank in 10 and 15 hours. Work in 1 hour:
A) 1/6
B) 1/5
C) 1/4
D) 1/3
Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

10. If combined rate is 1/12, time taken is:
A) 12 hours
B) 10 hours
C) 15 hours
D) 20 hours
Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

11. Which method is best for solving pipe problems?
A) Percentage method
B) LCM method
C) Ratio method only
D) Square method
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

12. A tank is filled by pipe A in 9 hours and emptied by pipe B in 18 hours. Net time:
A) 18 hours
B) 9 hours
C) 12 hours
D) 6 hours
Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

13. If outlet is faster than inlet, tank will:
A) Fill
B) Empty
C) Stay same
D) Overflow
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

14. A fills in 5 hours, B fills in 10 hours, C empties in 20 hours. Net rate:
A) 1/4
B) 1/5
C) 1/6
D) 1/8
Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

15. Pipes and Cisterns is based on:
A) Speed and distance
B) Profit and loss
C) Time and work

Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

16. If A fills in 12 hours, in 3 hours work done is:
A) 1/3
B) 1/4

D) 1/6

--------------------------------------------------

17. Combined rate is always:
A) Product of rates
B) Sum of rates
C) Difference of rates only
D) Square of rates
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

18. Leak in a tank acts like:
A) Inlet
B) Outlet
C) No effect

--------------------------------------------------

19. A fills in 10 hours, B empties in 20 hours. Net time is:
A) 20 hours
B) 15 hours

--------------------------------------------------

20. First step in pipe problems is:
A) Add hours
B) Find rate
