PIPES AND CISTERNS - ADVANCED MCQ QUESTIONS

1. Pipe A can fill a tank in 12 hours. Pipe B can empty the same tank in 18 hours. If both are opened together, the tank will be filled in:
A) 36 hours
B) 24 hours
C) 36/5 hours
D) 36/7 hours
Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

2. Three pipes A, B and C can fill a tank in 10, 15, and 20 hours respectively. If A and B are open for 3 hours and then C is opened with A alone for the remaining time, the tank will be filled in:
A) 5 hours
B) 6 hours
C) 7 hours
D) 8 hours
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

3. Pipe A fills a cistern in 8 hours, and pipe B fills it in 12 hours. A leak can empty the cistern in 24 hours. If all three are opened, the cistern will be filled in:
A) 16 hours
B) 12 hours
C) 9.6 hours
D) 10 hours
Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

4. A pipe fills a tank in 18 hours. After 6 hours, a second pipe that empties in 12 hours is opened. The tank will be completely full after:
A) 18 hours
B) 21 hours
C) 24 hours
D) 20 hours
Answer: D

--------------------------------------------------

5. A cistern has two inlet pipes A and B and one outlet pipe C. A fills in 6 hours, B fills in 8 hours, and C empties in 16 hours. If all pipes are opened together, the cistern will be filled in:
A) 12 hours
B) 14 hours
C) 13 1/3 hours
D) 10 2/3 hours
Answer: D

--------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------

B) 9 hours
C) 10 hours
