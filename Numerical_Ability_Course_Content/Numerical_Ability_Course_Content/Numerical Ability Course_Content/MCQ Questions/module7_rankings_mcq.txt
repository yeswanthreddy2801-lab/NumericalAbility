RANKINGS - MCQ QUESTIONS

--------------------------------------------------

1. Ranking is based on:
A) Speed
B) Position
C) Weight
D) Age
Answer: B

2. Total formula is:
A) L + R + 1
B) L + R - 1
C) L × R
D) L - R
Answer: B

3. Ranking problems are:
A) Random
B) Position-based
C) Algebra only
D) Geometry
Answer: B

4. If 3rd from left and 5th from right, total:
A) 6
B) 7
C) 8
D) 9
Answer: B

5. Ranking uses:
A) Probability
B) Linear arrangement
C) Graphs
D) Ratios
Answer: B

6. Left rank means:
A) Right side position
B) Left side position
C) Middle
D) Random
Answer: B

7. Right rank means:
A) Left side
B) Right side
C) Center
D) None
Answer: B

8. Total persons formula gives:
A) Distance
B) Total count
C) Weight
D) Time
Answer: B

9. Ranking is useful in:
A) Exams
B) Games only
C) Physics
D) Chemistry
Answer: A

10. Position problems need:
A) Guessing
B) Logic
C) Drawing
D) Random choice
Answer: B
11.If a person is 4th from left and 7th from right, total number of persons is:
A) 10
B) 11
C) 12
D) 13
Answer: B
12.If total persons = L + R − 1, this formula is used when:
A) No overlapping position
B) Same person counted twice
C) Random arrangement
D) Circular arrangement
Answer: A
13.If a person moves one position forward in ranking, his rank:
A) Increases by 1
B) Decreases by 1
C) Becomes zero
D) Remains same
Answer: B
14.In ranking, “from left” means counting starts from:
A) Right end
B) Left end
C) Center
D) Any random point
Answer: B
15.In ranking problems, “from right” means counting starts from:
A) Left end
B) Right end
C) Middle
D) Top
Answer: B
16.If a person is first from left, his rank from right is:
A) Total − 1
B) Total
C) 1
D) 0
Answer: B
17.If two people exchange positions, ranking will:
A) Change
B) Stay same
C) Become zero
D) Double
Answer: A
18.Ranking problems are mainly solved using:
A) Arithmetic only
B) Logical reasoning
C) Physics formulas
D) Chemistry equations
Answer: B
19.Middle position in ranking is found using:
A) (L + R) / 2
B) (Total + 1) / 2
C) L × R
D) L − R
Answer: B
20.Ranking problems are common in:
A) Competitive exams
B) Cooking
C) Sports only
D) Biology
Answer: A
