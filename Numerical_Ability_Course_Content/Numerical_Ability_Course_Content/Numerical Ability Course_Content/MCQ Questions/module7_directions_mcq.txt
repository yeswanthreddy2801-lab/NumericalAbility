DIRECTIONS - ADVANCED MCQ QUESTIONS

1. A person starts facing North, turns right, walks 10 m, turns left and walks 15 m, then turns left again and walks 10 m. What direction is he facing now?
A) North
B) South
C) East
D) West
Answer: D

--------------------------------------------------

2. A walker faces East and turns left, then right, then right again. Which direction does he face?
A) North
B) South
C) East
D) West
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

3. A person walks 20 m North, 15 m East, 20 m South, and then 15 m West. Where is he now relative to his start?
A) 15 m East
B) 15 m West
C) At the starting point
D) 20 m North
Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

4. A man starts from point A and walks East 30 m, turns right and walks 20 m, then turns right and walks 30 m. In which direction is he from point A?
A) North
B) South
C) East
D) West
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

5. A person goes 5 km South, then 3 km West, then 5 km North. How far and in which direction is he from the starting point?
A) 3 km West
B) 3 km East
C) 10 km West
D) 5 km South
Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

6. A person starts facing South, makes two consecutive left turns, and then a right turn. Which direction is he facing?

B) South
C) East
D) West

--------------------------------------------------

7. A person walks 10 m East, turns left, walks 6 m, turns left again, walks 10 m, and finally turns right. Which direction is he facing?

D) West

--------------------------------------------------
