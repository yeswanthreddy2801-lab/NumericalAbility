DIRECTIONS - MCQ QUESTIONS

--------------------------------------------------

1. North means:
A) Down
B) Up
C) Left
D) Right
Answer: B

2. South means:
A) Up
B) Down
C) Left
D) Right
Answer: B

3. East means:
A) Left
B) Right
C) Up
D) Down
Answer: B

4. West means:
A) Right
B) Left
C) Up
D) Down
Answer: B

5. Right turn is:
A) Anti-clockwise
B) Clockwise
C) Straight
D) None
Answer: B

6. Left turn is:
A) Clockwise
B) Anti-clockwise
C) Straight
D) None
Answer: B

7. Directions are based on:
A) Movement
B) Weight
C) Speed
D) Time
Answer: A

8. Best method is:
A) Drawing diagram
B) Guessing
C) Writing only
D) Ignoring
Answer: A

9. Final position depends on:
A) Random
B) Direction + distance
C) Speed
D) Weight
Answer: B

10. Directions are used in:
A) Exams
B) Cooking
C) Drawing
D) Music
Answer: A
11.If a person moves North and then turns right, the new direction is:
A) West
B) East
C) South
D) North
Answer: B
12.If a person faces East and turns left, he will face:
A) North
B) South
C) West
D) East
Answer: A
13.Opposite direction of North is:
A) East
B) West
C) South
D) North-East
Answer: C
14.Opposite direction of East is:
A) West
B) North
C) South
D) East
Answer: A
15.If a person turns 180°, the direction becomes:
A) Same direction
B) Opposite direction
C) Right turn
D) Left turn
Answer: B
16.One full rotation in direction problems is:
A) 90°
B) 180°
C) 270°
D) 360°
Answer: D
17.If a person moves South, then West, the final direction is:
A) South-West
B) North-West
C) North-East
D) South-East
Answer: A
18.In direction problems, movement is usually represented using:
A) Letters
B) Coordinates or diagram
C) Colors
D) Numbers only
Answer: B
19.If a person faces North and turns right twice, he will face:
A) South
B) West
C) East
D) North
Answer: B
20.Direction problems are best solved using:
A) Memory
B) Logical diagram method
C) Guessing
D) Formula only
Answer: B
