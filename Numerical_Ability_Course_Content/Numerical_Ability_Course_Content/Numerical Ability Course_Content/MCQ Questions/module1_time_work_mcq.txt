TIME AND WORK - MCQ QUESTIONS

1. A can complete a work in 10 days. How much work does he complete in one day?

A) 1/5
B) 1/10
C) 10
D) 5

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

2. B completes a work in 20 days. His one day's work is:

A) 1/10
B) 1/20
C) 20
D) 2

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

3. A completes a work in 12 days and B in 18 days. Together they complete it in:

A) 7.2 days
B) 8 days
C) 9 days
D) 10 days

Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

4. A completes a work in 8 days and B in 24 days. Together they finish the work in:

A) 5 days
B) 6 days
C) 8 days
D) 4 days

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

5. If A is twice as efficient as B and B takes 20 days, A takes:

A) 5 days
B) 8 days
C) 10 days
D) 15 days

Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

6. If efficiency ratio is 4:5, then time ratio is:

A) 4:5
B) 5:4
C) 1:4
D) 9:5

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

7. Total work should preferably be assumed as:

A) Prime Number
B) LCM
C) HCF
D) Square

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

8. Work is equal to:

A) Time ÷ Rate
B) Rate × Time
C) Time × Efficiency
D) Work ÷ Time

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

9. A and B together complete a work in 6 days. A alone takes 10 days. B alone takes:

A) 12
B) 15
C) 18
D) 20

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

10. If one day's work is 1/25, total days required are:

A) 5
B) 10
C) 20
D) 25

Answer: D

--------------------------------------------------

11. Which quantity is inversely proportional to efficiency?

A) Work
B) Time
C) Wage
D) Profit

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

12. Two workers complete a work in 4 days. If one worker alone takes 12 days, the other takes:

A) 4
B) 5
C) 6
D) 8

Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

13. If A can do a work in 15 days, then in 5 days he completes:

A) 1/5
B) 1/3
C) 2/5
D) 3/5

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

14. Which method reduces calculations?

A) Decimal Method

B) LCM Method

C) Square Method

D) Cube Method

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

15. If three workers have daily work rates 1/10, 1/15 and 1/30, together they complete the work in:

A) 5 days

B) 6 days

C) 8 days

D) 10 days

Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

16. If work increases and efficiency remains constant, time will:

A) Increase

B) Decrease

C) Remain same

D) Become zero

Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

17. One day's work of a person taking N days is:

A) N

B) 1/N

C) N²

D) N/2

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

18. If A:B efficiency ratio is 3:2, then time ratio is:

A) 3:2

B) 2:3

C) 5:3

D) 3:5

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

19. Which topic is closely related to Time and Work?

A) Pipes and Cisterns

B) Calendar

C) Clocks

D) Probability

Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

20. The first step in solving Time and Work problems is:

A) Add the days

B) Find one day's work

C) Multiply efficiencies

D) Find profit

Answer: B
