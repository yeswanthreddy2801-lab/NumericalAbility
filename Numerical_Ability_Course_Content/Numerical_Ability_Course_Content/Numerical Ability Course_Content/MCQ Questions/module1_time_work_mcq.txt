TIME AND WORK - ADVANCED MCQ QUESTIONS

1. A worker A can complete a job in 12 days and B can complete the same job in 18 days. If they work together for 4 days and then A leaves, the remaining work is finished by B in:
A) 6 days
B) 9 days
C) 12 days
D) 15 days
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

2. A does 1/5 of a work in 3 days and B does 1/6 of the same work in 2 days. Working together, they will complete the work in:
A) 10 days
B) 9 days
C) 8 days
D) 7 days
Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

3. A can do a piece of work in 20 days and B in 30 days. If they work alternately starting with A, how many days will they take to finish the work?
A) 14 days
B) 15 days
C) 16 days
D) 17 days
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

4. A takes 6 days more than B to finish a job. Together they finish in 8 days. The time taken by B alone is:
A) 10 days
B) 12 days
C) 14 days
D) 16 days
Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

5. Three workers A, B and C can finish a work in 10, 15 and 20 days respectively. If A works alone for 2 days and then B and C finish the rest together, the total time taken is:
A) 6 days
B) 7 days
C) 8 days
D) 9 days
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

6. Workers A and B together can complete a job in 9 days. If A alone is twice as fast as B, then A alone will take:
A) 12 days
B) 15 days
C) 18 days
D) 21 days
Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

7. A can finish a work in 24 days, B in 30 days and C in 40 days. If all three work together and C leaves after 4 days, the remaining work is completed by A and B in:
A) 5 days
B) 6 days
C) 7 days
D) 8 days
Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

8. A worker can complete 2/5 of a work in 6 days. How many days will he take to finish the entire work at the same rate?
A) 10 days
B) 12 days

D) 18 days
Answer: D

--------------------------------------------------

9. A does 1/3 of a work in 4 days, B does 1/4 of it in 5 days. How long will they take together to finish the whole work?
A) 10 days
B) 12 days
C) 14 days
D) 15 days
Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

10. A machine can finish a job in 12 hours and another identical machine can finish it in 18 hours. If both run together for 4 hours and then one machine continues alone, the remaining job is completed in:
A) 3 hours
B) 4 hours

D) 6 hours

--------------------------------------------------
