1. Permutation means:
A) Selection
B) Arrangement
C) Grouping

Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

2. Combination means:
A) Arrangement
B) Selection

--------------------------------------------------

3. Permutation formula:
A) nCr
B) n!/(n−r)!

--------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------
