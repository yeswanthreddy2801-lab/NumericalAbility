PROBABILITY - COINS & DICE MCQs

--------------------------------------------------

1. Probability formula is:
A) Total/Favorable
B) Favorable/Total
C) Total + Favorable
D) Total - Favorable
Answer: B

2. One coin has:
A) 1 outcome
B) 2 outcomes
C) 3 outcomes
D) 4 outcomes
Answer: B

3. Two coins total outcomes:
A) 2
B) 3
C) 4
D) 6
Answer: C

4. One dice has:
A) 4 outcomes
B) 5 outcomes
C) 6 outcomes
D) 7 outcomes
Answer: C

5. Two dice total outcomes:
A) 12
B) 18
C) 24
D) 36
Answer: D

6. Probability value always lies between:
A) 0 to 2
B) 0 to 1
C) 1 to 10
D) -1 to 1
Answer: B

7. Probability of sure event:
A) 0
B) 1
C) 0.5
D) 2
Answer: B

8. Probability of impossible event:
A) 0
B) 1
C) 0.5
D) -1
Answer: A

9. Probability of head in one coin:
A) 1/3
B) 1/2
C) 1/4
D) 2/3
Answer: B

10. Outcomes of two dice:
A) 12
B) 18
C) 36
D) 24
Answer: C

11. Total outcomes of n coins:
A) n
B) 2n
C) 2^n
D) n^2
Answer: C

12. Total outcomes of n dice:
A) 6^n
B) n^6
C) 6n
D) n^2
Answer: A

13. Sum 7 in dice has probability:
A) 1/12
B) 1/6
C) 1/36
D) 1/18
Answer: B

14. Sample space means:
A) Favorable outcomes
B) Total outcomes
C) Random guess
D) None
Answer: B

15. At least problems are solved using:
A) Addition
B) Complement method
C) Multiplication only
D) Division only
Answer: B
16. Q: In two dice, probability of getting doublets (same number on both dice) is:
A) 1/6
B) 1/12
C) 1/36
D) 1/18
Answer: A
