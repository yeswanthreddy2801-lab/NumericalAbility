MAN, CHAIN RULE AND WAGES - ADVANCED MCQ QUESTIONS

1. A and B together can complete a job in 12 days. A alone takes 20 days. How many days does B alone take?
A) 15 days
B) 18 days
C) 30 days
D) 24 days
Answer: D

--------------------------------------------------

2. If 5 men can do a piece of work in 8 days, how many men are required to finish it in 5 days, assuming the work done per man per day is constant?
A) 8
B) 7
C) 6
D) 5
Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------

3. A can do a piece of work in 12 days and B can do it in 16 days. If they work on alternate days starting with A, the work is completed in:
A) 8 days
B) 9 days
C) 10 days
D) 11 days
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

4. 3 men and 5 boys can do a job in 10 days, while 5 men and 3 boys can do it in 7 days. How many days will 4 men and 4 boys take?
A) 8 days
B) 8 4/7 days
C) 9 days
D) 7 1/2 days
Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

5. A worker is paid Rs. 300 for 15 days of work. If the same worker is paid Rs. 240 for 12 days, how much will he be paid for 10 days of work?
A) Rs. 200
B) Rs. 210
C) Rs. 220
D) Rs. 240
Answer: A

--------------------------------------------------
